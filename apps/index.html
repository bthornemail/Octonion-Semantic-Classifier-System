üîê Our Privacy Guarantee

All analysis happens in your browser. We never collect, store, or transmit your data. Every application in this suite runs entirely client-side using WebAssembly and the Transformers.js library.

You own your data. You control your data. We never see your data.

All source code is open and auditable. All methodology is transparent and reproducible.

These applications use a novel approach to semantic text classification combining:

The result: A system that can analyze the structure of information, not just the content.
